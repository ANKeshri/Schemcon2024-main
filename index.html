About the conference

About SCHEMCON

SCHEMCON, Students’ Chemical Engineering Congress, is an annual event organized by the Students’ Chapter of Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers (IIChE) under one of its Regional Centers. It serves as a pivotal platform for Chemical Engineering students to engage, learn, and immerse themselves in the dynamic landscape of their field. SCHEMCON-2024 marks the 20th edition of this significant gathering, jointly organized by the Department of Chemical Engineering at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, Jais and the Amethi Regional Centre of the Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers. The overarching theme of SCHEMCON 2024 revolves around ‘The future of the new paradigms of Chemical Engineering’. Set in a premier institution for Chemical Engineering education, this event promises to be distinctive in its breadth of perspectives and innovative ideas, drawing participation from industry leaders, researchers, educators, and talented students across diverse disciplines

About Organising Institute

Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT), in Jais, Amethi (formerly in Raebareli), Uttar Pradesh, India, is an Institute of National Importance established along the lines of IITs.It is a unique academic and research institution as it is the first autonomous institution established under the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas (MoPNG), Government of India, conferring undergraduate and postgraduate degrees. RGIPT is a training and education institute focusing on STEM and petroleum industry. It has also started as an energy specific institute and now it is expanding its horizons in other fields as well. It is an institute with a well established background in the field of engineering and research with a NIRF ranking of 79 in 2023.

About IIChE

Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers (IIChE) was established on May 18, 1947 in Jadavpur University, Kolkata by Dr. Hira Lal Roy and the place is recognised as the headquarters of IIChE since then. The aim of IIChE is to spread Chemical Engineering education and to foster the interest of the Chemical Engineering profession in general. At present, IIChE has around 29,000 members, 42 Regional Centres and more than 165 Student Chapters. The Institute is recognized by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India, as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization. IIChE annually organize Indian Chemical Engineering Congress (CHEMCON). IIChE has been organizing SCHEMCON (Students' CHEMCON) annually since 2005.

About IIChE Amethi Regional Center

IIChE-Amethi Regional Centre (ARC) was established in December 2021 at the premises of Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, Jais which falls under Amethi District. Presently, the RGIPT has 18 faculties as its members and which is supposed to grow further. IIChE-ARC is committed to promoting the advancements in Chemical Engineering profession by organizing meetings, conferences, seminars and workshops. Refresher courses and counselling sessions for the students shall also be organised to inculcate scientific temper in them.

About CBE Department

Step into the realm of Chemical and Biochemical Engineering at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, Jais, where the convergence of science, technology, and innovation defines our mission. Our department is a dynamic hub of exploration and discovery, where students engage in cutting-edge research and experiential learning under the guidance of well-experienced faculty. With a curriculum designed to blend theoretical rigor with hands-on experience, our students are equipped to tackle the complex challenges of today and tomorrow. From developing sustainable energy solutions to advancing biomedical technologies, we empower our graduates to make a lasting impact on society and shape the future of engineering.

About MP Chary Award

IIChE has also been felicitating M.P. Chary Award annually to an outstanding Chemical Engineer below 35 years of age or pursuing PhD or competed PhD in last year. The candidate will be required to make a presentation on his/her research work or his/her view on selected topic. The award carries M.P. Chary Medal and a prize money of Rs. 10,000/- . The template for the same is provided on the IIChE website (https://iiche.org.in/).
