About the conference

About SCHEMCON

SCHEMCON, Students Chemical Engineering Congress, is an annual event organized by the Students Chapter of Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers (IIChE) under one of its Regional Centers. It serves as a pivotal platform for Chemical Engineering students to engage, learn, and immerse themselves in the dynamic landscape of their field. SCHEMCON-2024 marks the 20th edition of this significant gathering, jointly organized by the Department of Chemical Engineering at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, Jais and the Amethi Regional Centre of the Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers. The overarching theme of SCHEMCON 2024 revolves around ‘New paradigms of Chemical Engineering’. Set in a premier institution for Chemical Engineering education, this event promises to be distinctive in its breadth of perspectives and innovative ideas, drawing participation from industry leaders, researchers, educators, and talented students across diverse disciplines

About Organising Institute

The Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology Jais, Amethi, Uttar Pradesh was established by the Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas (MoPNG), Government of India by an Act of the Parliament in 2007. The institute was accorded with the status of an Institution of National Importance and its NIRF ranking in 2023 is 79th in engineering category. The institute is co-promoted as an energy domain specific institute by six leading oil public sector units (ONGC, IOCL, OIL, GAIL, BPCL, and HPCL) in association with the OIDB. The institute is associated with the leading International Universities/Institutions specializing in the domain of petroleum, petrochemicals, and energy sectors. There are 7 departments and 2 centers offering 13 UG and 6 PG programs and doctoral programs in STEM.

About IIChE

Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers (IIChE) was established on May 18, 1947 in Jadavpur University, Kolkata by Dr. Hira Lal Roy and the place is recognised as the headquarters of IIChE since then. The aim of IIChE is to spread Chemical Engineering education and to foster the interest of the Chemical Engineering profession in general. At present, IIChE has around 29,000 members, 42 Regional Centres and more than 165 Student Chapters. The Institute is recognized by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India, as a Scientific and Industrial Research Organization. IIChE annually organize Indian Chemical Engineering Congress (CHEMCON). IIChE has been organizing SCHEMCON (Students' CHEMCON) annually since 2005.

About IIChE Amethi Regional Center

IIChE-Amethi Regional Centre (ARC) was established in December 2021 at the premises of Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, Jais which falls under Amethi District. Presently, the IIChE-ARC has 19 faculty members and growing. IIChE-ARC is committed to promoting the advancements in Chemical Engineering profession by organizing meetings, conferences, seminars and workshops. About CBE Department

Step into the realm of Chemical and Biochemical Engineering at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology, Jais, where the convergence of science, technology, and innovation defines our mission. Our department is a dynamic hub of exploration and discovery, where students engage in cutting-edge research and experiential learning under the guidance of well-experienced faculty. With a curriculum designed to blend theoretical rigor with hands-on experience, our students are equipped to tackle the complex challenges of today and tomorrow. From developing sustainable energy solutions to advancing biomedical technologies, we empower our graduates to make a lasting impact on society and shape the future of engineering.

About MP Chary Award

IIChE has also been felicitating M.P. Chary Award annually to an outstanding Chemical Engineer below 35 years of age or pursuing PhD or competed PhD in last year. The candidate will be required to make a presentation on his/her research work or his/her view on selected topic. The award carries M.P. Chary Medal and a prize money of Rs. 10,000/- .The template for the same is provided on the IIChE website Visit Here.
